CATALUNYA

INTRODUCTION

Catalunya.com is a website created to attract turism to Catalonia. Offering a lot of activities, events and routes adapting this range of options to all type of people. Also wants to help the user offering the oportunity of buy experience at the moment with the platform “Experience Catalunya”.

OBJECTIVE

The objective of this project was:
·Design a new information arquitecture proposal
·Design a new visual propopsal
·Responsive design it for Desktop and Mobile

  • ROLE
  • TEAM
  • DURATION
  • UX/UI Designer
  • Marc Martin (Solo)
  • 1 month 2020

PROBLEM

I had 4 points to cover:
·Overinformation: Seems like they wanted to show a lot of information to the user, but that is overwhelming and unuseful
·Order: It’s really hard to find what you want
·Searcher: is poorly understood
·Information repeated

ARCHITECTURE

I changed the architecture to order all the information starting with the menu. I separated the menu by epics:
·What to see
·Where to go
·Activities
·Organize your trip

WIREFRAMES

This wireframes helped me to start with the guerrilla testing to validate the functionalities and detect some issues with the interactions. After these testings I iterated the wireframes and added some colors and text style.

CONCLUSION

Redesign this web it was a challenge because it’s destinated to all kind of people and ages who wants to visit Catalonia. This has generated me a lot of doubts, but at least I decided to implement a useful and understand design following the government desgin line but with a modern touch.
